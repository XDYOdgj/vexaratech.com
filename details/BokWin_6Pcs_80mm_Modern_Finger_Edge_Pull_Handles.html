BokWin 80mm Finger Edge Pull Handles - Modern and Durable

Elevate the style of your kitchen, bedroom, or bathroom cabinets with the sleek and modern design of BokWin 80mm finger edge pull handles. These high-quality handles are made from durable aluminum alloy, ensuring long-lasting performance and a smooth, injury-free surface. The hidden edge feature not only adds a clean, seamless look but also eliminates sharp corners, making them safe for homes with children and pets.

Designed for ease of use, these pulls come with pre-drilled holes and all necessary screws, simplifying installation on doors, drawers, and cupboards. Their universal fit means they work well in a variety of settings, from kitchens to living rooms and even wardrobes. Whether you're updating your existing cabinets or installing new ones, these BokWin pulls are the perfect solution for a modern, functional furniture upgrade.
