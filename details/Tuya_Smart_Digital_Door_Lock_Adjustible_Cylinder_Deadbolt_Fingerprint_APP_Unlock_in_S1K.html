Tuya Smart Digital Door Lock

Enhance your home security with a modern, convenient solution designed for every need.

Smart and Secure Access

The Tuya Smart Digital Door Lock is your perfect partner in modern home security. With multiple unlock options, including fingerprint recognition, password input, APP remote control, and IC card, you can access your space with ease and confidence whether you're home or away. This versatile lock adapts to different door thicknesses thanks to its adjustable cylinder length, making installation effortless and tailored to your home.

Experience quick, accurate, and secure entry using the high-precision fingerprint sensor. The Tuya APP lets you manage access remotely, set permissions, and monitor your door from anywhere, giving you peace of mind no matter where you are.

Built for durability, the deadbolt is designed with anti-theft features to protect your home or office. Whether you're on a busy schedule or looking for a reliable lock solution, the Tuya Smart Digital Door Lock provides the security and convenience you need.

Key Features
  • Multi-function unlock modes: fingerprint, password, APP, and IC card
  • Adjustable cylinder length for different door thickness
  • High-precision fingerprint recognition, fast and secure
  • Remote APP management for opening and security settings
  • Durable deadlock design with anti-theft protection

Each feature is designed to improve your daily experience while ensuring your home stays safe and secure.
